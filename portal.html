How to Play

Master the basics in 30 seconds

1

Pick a Block

Choose from the three blocks shown below the grid. Each has a different shape and color.

2

Drag & Place

Drag the block onto the grid. A ghost preview shows where it'll land. Release to place it.

3

Clear Lines

Fill any complete row or column to clear it and score points. Clear multiple lines for combos!

4

Don't Get Stuck

The game ends when none of the remaining blocks can fit on the grid. Plan ahead!
